Casting a spell with the scepter costs 4 [experience](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Experience) points.

## Infusion
The blaze scepter can be obtained by taking damage from a [blaze](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Blaze) to infuse a held [magical sceper](../items/magical_scepter).

### Attack - [Fire Charges](../spells/spells#fire_charges)
Shoot three [small fireballs](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Small_Fireball) seperated each by 6 ticks with a casting cooldown of 2 seconds.

### Protect - [Fire Resistance](../spells/spells#fire_resistance)
Get [fire resistance](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Fire_Resistance) for 10 to 12.5 seconds with a casting cooldown of 2 seconds.
