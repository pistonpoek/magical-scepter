The spell wither skull is a projectile that resembles the attack of a [wither](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Wither).

## Occurrence

The [wither skull](../spells/spells#wither_skull) spell will shoot a spell wither skull.

## Damage

When the spell wither skull will explode on collision and deal wither damage, but does not destroy blocks.
