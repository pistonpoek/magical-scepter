The spell guardian beam is a charging laser that resembles the attack of a [guardian](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Guardian) and will deal damage after warming up for some seconds.

## Occurrence

The [guardian beam](../spells/spells#guardian_beam) spell will shoot a spell guardian beam.

## Damage

After charging up for some seconds the spell guardian beam will deal 6 points of damage.

## Color

The spell guardian beam is colored based on th and linearly transition from #402080 to #FFDF40 during the warmup.
When the entity is on their last tick it will be colored #00E040.
