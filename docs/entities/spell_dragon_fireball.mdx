The spell dragon fireball is a projectile that resembles the attack of a [ender dragon](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Ender_Dragon).

## Occurrence

The [dragon fireball](../spells/spells#dragon_fireball) spell will shoot a spell dragon fireball.

## Damage

When the spell dragon fireball will explode on collision and leave some lingering cloud that deals damage.
