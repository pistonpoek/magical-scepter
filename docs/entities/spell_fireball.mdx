The spell fireball is a projectile that resembles the attack of a [ghast](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Ghast).

## Occurrence

The [ghast fireball](../spells/spells#ghast_fireball) spell will shoot a spell fireball.

## Damage

When the spell fireball will explode on collision and deal damage, but does not destroy blocks.
