The spell fire charge is a projectile that resembles the attack of a [blaze](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Blaze).

## Occurrence

The [blaze fire charges](../spells/spells#blaze_fire_charges) spell will shoot a spell fire charge.

## Damage

When the spell fire charge will explode on collision and deal damage, but does not destroy blocks.
