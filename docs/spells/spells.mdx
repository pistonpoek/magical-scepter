---
title: Spells
---

Spells can be cast with [magical scepters](../items/magical_scepter) or through the use of the [/spell](spell_command) command.
Spells are data driven and [defined](spell_definition) in json and perform effects for a living entity when cast. 
There are 20 spells found in the mod that can each be cast using a corresponding scepter and action.

### <span id="blaze_fire_charges">Blaze Fire Charges</span>
Attack with the [blaze scepter](../scepters/blaze_scepter) to shoot three [spell fire charges](../entities/spell_fire_charge) separated each by 6 ticks.

### <span id="fire_resistance">Fire Resistance</span>
Use the [blaze scepter](../scepters/blaze_scepter) to get [fire resistance](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Fire_Resistance) for 10 to 12.5 seconds.


### <span id="wind_charge">Wind Charge</span>
Attack with the [breeze scepter](../scepters/breeze_scepter) to shoot a [wind charge](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Wind_Charge).

### <span id="breeze_jump">Breeze Jump</span>
Use the [breeze scepter](../scepters/breeze_scepter) to make a high jump from on the ground or get [slow falling](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Slow_Falling) for 0.8 to 1.2 seconds.


### <span id="dragon_fireball">Dragon Fireball</span>
Attack with the [dragon scepter](../scepters/dragon_scepter) to shoot a [spell dragon fireball](../entities/spell_dragon_fireball).

### <span id="dragon_growl">Dragon Growl</span>
Use the [dragon scepter](../scepters/dragon_scepter) to spray damaging dragon breath 4 times nearby on the ground separated by 4 ticks.


### <span id="evoker_fang_line">Evoker Fang Line</span>
Attack with the [evoker scepter](../scepters/evoker_scepter) to summon a line of 16 [evoker fangs](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Evoker#Evoker_fangs).

### <span id="evoker_fang_circle">Evoker Fang Circle</span>
Use the [evoker scepter](../scepters/evoker_scepter) to summon 2 circles of 5 and 8 [evoker fangs](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Evoker#Evoker_fangs) respectively.


### <span id="ghast_fireball">Ghast Fireball</span>
Attack with the [ghast scepter](../scepters/ghast_scepter) to shoot a [spell fireball](../entities/spell_fireball).

### <span id="regeneration">Regeneration</span>
Use the [ghast scepter](../scepters/ghast_scepter) to get [regeneration](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Regeneration) II for 10 to 12.5 seconds.


### <span id="guardian_beam">Guardian Beam</span>
Attack with the [guardian scepter](../scepters/guardian_scepter) to shoot a [spell guardian beam](../entities/spell_guardian_beam).

### <span id="haste">Haste</span>
Use the [guardian scepter](../scepters/guardian_scepter) to get [haste](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Haste) II for 20 to 25 seconds and removes [mining fatigue](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Mining_Fatigue) effect.


### <span id="magical_attack">Magical Attack</span>
Attack with the [magical scepter](../scepters/magical_scepter) to shoot a ray that deals 5 points of indirect magic damage when hit.

### <span id="resistance">Resistance</span>
Use the [magical scepter](../scepters/magical_scepter) to get [resistance](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Resistance) II for 10 to 12.5 seconds.


### <span id="shulker_bullet">Shulker Bullet</span>
Attack with the [shulker scepter](../scepters/shulker_scepter) to shoot a [shulker bullet](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Shulker_Bullet) at the facing target.

### <span id="teleport">Teleport</span>
Use the [shulker scepter](../scepters/shulker_scepter) to teleport to a random 'safe' place in a 8x4x8 area around the caster.


### <span id="sonic_boom">Sonic Boom</span>
Attack with the [warden scepter](../scepters/warden_scepter) to blast a sonic boom at a target dealing 10 points of damage and significant knockback.

### <span id="stability">Stability</span>
Use the [warden scepter](../scepters/warden_scepter) to get [stability](../effects/stability) II for 20 to 25 seconds. 


### <span id="wither_skull">Wither Skull</span>
Attack with the [wither scepter](../scepters/wither_scepter) to shoot a [spell wither skull](../entities/spell_wither_skull).

### <span id="repulsion">Repulsion</span>
Use the [wither scepter](../scepters/wither_scepter) to get [repulsion](../effects/repulsion) for 10 to 12.5 seconds.
