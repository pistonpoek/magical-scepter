An old taiga cabin is a small [structure][structure] with the ability to spawn [sorcerers](../entities/sorcerer).

## <span id="generation">Generation</span>

Old taiga cabins generate in the [old growth pine taiga][old-growth-pine-taiga] and [old growth spruce taiga][old-growth-spruce-taiga] biomes.

## <span id="structure">Structure</span>

An old taiga cabin is a small accomodation made of local materials. 
The floor forms a rectangular space and consists of a mix of [cobblestone](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Cobblestone) and [mossy cobblestone](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Mossy_Cobblestone). 
The walls are made of [spruce planks](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Planks#Spruce) with [spruce logs](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Log#Spruce) as pillars at the corners. 
On top of the walls is a diagonal roof made of [spruce log](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Log#Spruce) beams. 
The building has two side windows, a small back window filled with a [spruce fence](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Wooden_Fence#Spruce) and a doorway with a porch in front. 

Inside of the structure a [crafting table](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Crafting_Table) and [chest](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Chest) generated with [loot](#loot) are placed. One of the windows has a flower pot with a [brown mushroom](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Brown_Mushroom) inside of it.

## <span id="mobs">Mobs</span>

Upon world generation one [sorcerer](../entities/sorcerer) spawns inside the cabin and will never naturally despawn.
 
Furthermore, sorcerers can spawn at any block within the 7x7x9 bounding area of the structure.
