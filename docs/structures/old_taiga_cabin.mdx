An old taiga cabin is a small [structure][structure] with the ability to spawn [refractors](../entities/refractor).

## <span id="generation">Generation</span>

Old taiga cabins generate in the [old growth pine taiga][old-growth-pine-taiga] and [old growth spruce taiga][old-growth-spruce-taiga] biomes.
